# Final Project
- Involves indicating which lab test results are the most important when diagnosing chronic kidney disease.
- I used an unsupervised model, a blend of different models (ensemble), including a random forest model.
- A relatively high accuracy came from this model, around 97.5%. The cross-validation accuracy was also high, being 98.24%. The top five most important lab indicators for this model were specific gravity levels, hemoglobin levels, packed cell volumes, serum creatinine levels, and red blood cell counts.
