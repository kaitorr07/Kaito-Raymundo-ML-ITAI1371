# Midterm Project
- Involved creating an AI model that sorts autoimmune patients based on treatability and duration of symtpoms.
- I used a supervised model, specifically, a logistical regression model to sort through the patient data.
- The results were relatively accurate, with an average accuracy of around 89.88%. The cross-validation accuracy was reasonable as well, being 93%. This means that for the most part, the model performed well and correctly sorted almost all of the patients.
